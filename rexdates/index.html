Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to help everyone from newbie freshmen to the oldest of seniors at byui with where to go and opening up more possibilities in the dating game. We are not a dating site to help you find dates, rather a dating site to help you plan a great date. whether it's your first date or just looking to try something new

Audience

Our audience will be the students of Brigham Young University-Idaho. Namely the new freshmen that have not been here long and have yet to find the date spots, or hear of the many activities available. It will also target the older students who may just be looking to break themselves out of a rut of bad dates. Most if not all traffic will most likely come from phones.
