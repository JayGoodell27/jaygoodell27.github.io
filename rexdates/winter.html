Winter Ideas

Winter in Rexburg can be very harsh, it can sometimes be difficult to come up with ideas when everything is covered in snow or frozen over.

1. Laser tag at the MC

If you're looking for a fun and light first date, laser tag at the school is fun and cheap! To see when the laser tag date is click here!

2. School play/concert

The school puts on various concerts and plays throught the month, they are perfect for a colder day!

3. Fat Cats

Fat Cats holds many activities! Everything from golfing and minigolf, to arcade games and vr games. They also have a great theater. It is a great spot for the last minute date! You can see find prices and movie times here!

4. Gravity Factory

Gravity factory is a trampoline park that has become somewhat of a staple in Rexburg. Because it is so well known it is often busy. You will have to buy their socks, or if you or your date has already bought some you will want to bring them. They are required to have before you jump. Click here for more info.

5. Christmas light Drive

Jump in the car and take a scenic adventure through the suburbs of Rexburg and search for the houses with Christmas lights!

6. Skiing/ Snowboarding

There are quite a few places to go skiing and Snowboarding, even sledding. Places like the temple and campus, But for sure the most popular is Targhee ski resort. It is a bit removed from Rexy, but is the top spot for a great date!

7. Fire at the Dunes

If your looking for a more intimit date, taking some wood and blankets up to the dunes and cooking some smores or hot coco sets up the perfect mood for a romantic date.

8. Heise Hot Springs / lava hot springs

If you are looking for a break from the cold, a hot spring might be the best thing! If you are looking to are looking to cut the cold, but you dont have a way to get there, there are a few apartment complexes that have hottubs too.

9. D.I date

This one is better in a big group. Go to the D.I with your date and find outfits, the more ridiculous the better. Then wear your new outfits together while you eat out at a restaurant.
