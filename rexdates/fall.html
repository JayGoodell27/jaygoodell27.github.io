Fall Ideas

Fall is one of the best semesters for dating, it's when the campus is at its fullest. It's also some of the last weeks to take advantage of the outoor activities.

1. Take a hike!

There are many nature trails and hikes all around the area. R mountain is a great one to start with. For a better list of hikes, click here!

2. watch sunrise / sunset

Wether or not you do this on your hike, or just in the park, it is a great chance at a more romantic date. Take a blanket and some snacks with you and watch the sunrise or look at the stary constalations together!

3. Lego night

After dinner, go to the store and choose legos for eachother. Head back home and build them together.

4. Homemade pizza

Make pizzas together! Use the toppings and be creative! It could even be a dessert pizza.

5. Hallway minigolf

Get some red solo cups and set up your own mini golf course, you can get some small foam balls from a craft store and some cheap clubs from a secondhand store.

6. Aquarium

Visit the aquarium in Idaho Falls.

7. Heber Hatchets

Throw some hatchets and end the night with a meal.

8. Borrow bikes from the bike rack

Take some bikes from a bike rack and do a small tour around town, just remember to put them back!

9. Visit a haunted house

During the month of october there are many haunted attractions to go to. Go just you two or get a group date together!

10. Walmart Marco Polo

Get the cheapest walki talkis and hide in the store, take turns giving the weakest hints on where you are.

11. Workout together

Head to the campus gym together, or if your apartment has its own gym you could go there!
